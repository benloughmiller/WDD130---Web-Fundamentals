Uses For Oranges

The skin of an orange is where most of the vitamin C is contained. If you rub orange peels on your skin, it can help heal your skin. This tip is great for people with acne-prone or oily skin.

The natural oils in orange peels are a great natural cleaner. If you put the peels in a jar of vinegar for two weeks then strain the liquid, you will be left with a great smelling cleaner. This cleaner is especially great for cleaning sticker residue, since orange peels have natural oils that work on grime.

Add some orange peels into a pot filled with water with additional ingredients such as a bit of vanilla or cinnamon. If you bring this mixture to a simmer, it will bring a great smell to your home.

One medium orange can provide nearly all your recommended daily vitamin C intake. Vitamin C supports the immune system, reduces inflammation, and helps your body perform its basic functions. It also helps your body absorb iron, which is an essential nutrient.
